Services We Offer

From a 5lb Chihuahua to a 130lb Rhodesian Ridgeback,
we are here to make sure your pet gets the customized care you need!

Dog Walking

Working all day or planning on being away from the house all day? We provide dog walking services, starting at a minimum of 30 minutes.
Rates start at $15.50 per hour per dog.

Daycare & Boarding

Open from 7 am to 10pm PT, our home is available for group and private dog boarding and care.
Rates for daycare starting at $35 a day and over-night boarding starting at $55.

Airport Transportation

Coming or going from Tahoe? We are here to help. If you need help getting to or from the Reno or Sacramento airport, we can provide transportation.
Call for pricing and avaliability.
